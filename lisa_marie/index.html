GREETTINGS, SALAM AND WELCOME
TO LISA MARIE'S WEBSITE

Lisa Marie is an artist from Covington, Ky. She started sharing her work in 2009 at local open mics. She has since won many poetry slams including: "Covington City Lights Slam" 2009, "Wild Women of Poetry Slam" hosted by The University of Kentucky 2016, and "Metaphorically Speaking Battle of Ohio" 2015 and 2016. She has released two albums of poetry titled, "Guilty of Truth" in 2010 and "Waking Up for Dreamers" in 2012. Lisa also has enjoyed sharing her work with young students encouraging them to share their passions, pains, and stories through poetry.
