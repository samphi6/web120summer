LET'S GO SHOPPING, DARLINGS!

Considering one of the most popular buttons to roll around on any website designed, we must recognize the superb power behind the Purchase/ Check Out button. To obtain the magnificent magic that this button offers, we must understand it’s root; the shopping cart. “A shopping cart on an online retailers site is a piece of software that facilitates the purchase of a product or service. It accepts the customer’s payment and organizes the distribution of that information to the merchant, payment processor and other parties.” (Big Commerce essentials, 2013-2019) The shopping cart will record item information, provides an entry level of access to order, category and customer management. Lastly, the cart produces data, categories and site information for user display. The shopping cart provides everything the real-life shopping experience has to offer… well, besides the possibility of the rude clerk to consumer and vice versa interaction. So thrilling!

As with many extras we may want to introduce into our designed sites, there are a plethora of software providers to get us started. BigCommerce, Wix and Shopify are in the top echelon of developers offering a variety of features, styles and designs (Andre, Louie, 2019). Although we have these wonderful resources, we can create a simple shopping cart by utilizing the knowledge we’ve accessed through the strength of HTML, CSS, PHP, Flexbox and JavaScript. To reference w3schools.com, we can scoop up some basic code to create a responsive checkout form to get us started or where we can get a feel for what goes into creating such elements of a website. Personally, I could possibly see myself making use of the software discovered on the net. However, building my own personalized shopping cart is much more appealing, as I would want to introduce it into a site worked to represent me in another stylistic way.
